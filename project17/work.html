Self-protrait in a Straw Hat
by Louise Elisabenth Lebrun

The painting appears, after cleaning, to be an autograph replica of a picture, the original of which was painted in Brussels in 1782 in free imitation of Rubens's 'Chapeau du Paille', ehich LeBrun has seen in Antwerp. It was Exhibited in Paris in 1782 at the Salon de la Correspondance. LeBrun's originals is recorded in a private collection in France.
