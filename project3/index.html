Guangzhou is usually known as Cantons in the west. Guangzhou is located on the Pearl River about 75 miles north of Hong Kong and 145 miles north of Macau. Guangzhou is as sometimes known as “City of Rams” or “Sheaves of Rice City”sometimes based on the legend story. The city of Guangzhou is the capital city of Guangdong province and the third most-populous city in mainland China. Since 214 BC, the city has been a springboard for trading, cultural exchanges, and many revolutions and reforms, making it an unavoidable stopover to understand what shapes modern China.
